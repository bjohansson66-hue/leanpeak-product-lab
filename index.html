Inside LeanPeak Product Lab

One workspace that connects challenge framing, SBCE, and launch readiness.

Structure your development flow into clear modules: from understanding the problem to converging on robust concepts and preparing a confident launch.

Module 1 · Pretotyping

Challenge framing cockpit

Visualize value, key decisions, knowledge gaps, and problems with visual project objectives and opportunity maps. Capture learnings as you go so decisions stay anchored.

Module 2 · Set‑based

Concept option engine

Generate, combine, and compare 8–64 concept variants with feasibility scoring and simple side‑by‑side views for your team and sponsors.

Module 3 · Experimentation

3P experiment board

Plan pilots, track learning cycles, and link results directly to the concept set so you see which options earn the right to move forward.

Module 4 · Risk & flow

Risk radar and flow metrics

Map probability × consequence, flag system hotspots, and keep a living mitigation plan tied to each product option.

Module 5 · Launch

Launch readiness planner

Build an 8‑week ramp‑up for training, validation, and stakeholder communication so launch feels quiet and controlled, not heroic.

Coaching

Virtual Bengt inside every module

Get context‑aware nudges, template suggestions, and review prompts so teams learn lean product development while doing real work.
