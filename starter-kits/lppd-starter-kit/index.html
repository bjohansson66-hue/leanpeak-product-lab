What’s inside

  • 1
    What LPPD is. A one-page definition of Lean Product & Process Development as a systems approach — treating development not as execution but as a learning job: structured cycles of discovery and convergence that build knowledge faster than conventional stage-gate processes.
  • 2
    The six guiding principles. An illustrated summary of the six principles that orient every LPPD decision: from value stream thinking and set-based convergence to knowledge reuse and concurrent development.
  • 3
    A development value stream spine. A visual overview of how learning cycles plug into the development value stream — from discovery and concept to pilot and launch — showing where decisions live and how knowledge flows.
  • 4
    A minimal Obeya layout for development. A practical four-zone template for setting up a development Obeya: customer value and True North, set-based options, experiments and decisions, and flow and rhythm — enough to run weekly stand-ups and integration reviews from day one.
  • 5
    How to use this kit in 4–6 weeks. A short practice sequence for introducing LPPD to your team: week-by-week steps from first read-through and Obeya setup through to running your first real learning cycle.

Ready to go deeper? The full Lean PPD Playbook expands every section of this kit into actionable chapters with plays, tools, and real examples — from portfolio strategy and concept development through to 3P industrialisation and production launch.
